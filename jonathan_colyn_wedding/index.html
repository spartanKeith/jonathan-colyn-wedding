Our Story in Three Chapters


His Story. Her Story.

God's Story.

His Story

How God Prepared Ian

Before we met, God was teaching me to trust His timing instead of my own. I prayed that if He would bless me with a relationship, my first girlfriend would also be my last. That prayer became a covenant between God and me, so whenever there was an opportunity to pursue someone, He gave me the conviction to wait.

When I met Colyn, I admired her deeply. There were seasons when pursuing her became difficult, and I wanted to stop. God reminded me that my role was not simply to win her heart, but to faithfully reflect His love. Looking back, I see that He wasn't delaying His promise; He was preparing my heart to become the husband He was calling me to be.

Her Story

How God Pursued Colyn

We met in 2020 as coworkers. In 2021, Ian asked me, "What do you want in a relationship?" I answered that I wanted my next boyfriend to be my last and someone who would lead me closer to God. Ironically, my life wasn't aligned with that desire. I was searching for love in the wrong places and believed I was too far from God.

Instead of judging me, Ian became a faithful friend who listened, prayed, and pointed me to Christ. In February 2022, I surrendered my life to Jesus and realized something that changed everything: before Ian was pursuing me, Jesus had already been pursuing my heart. What I once thought was a delay was actually God's perfect timing.

God's Story

How God Wrote Our Story

God didn't bring us together when we expected. He brought us together when He knew we were ready.

During our personal daily devotions, God spoke to each of us separately. Ian received the conviction to pursue me again, while I received the conviction to say yes. Neither of us planned it, yet God was leading us in the same direction.

Years before we became a couple, Ian gave me a ring that I tucked away and forgot. When I found it again, I noticed the engraving:

"God's Timing."

Looking back, we realized those words described more than a ring—they described our story.

Our relationship wasn't founded on perfect timing alone; it was founded on obedience. We simply chose to follow where God was leading, and in His perfect time, He brought our stories together.

"He has made everything beautiful in its time." - Ecclesiastes 3:11
